Student Review Committee (SRC) Funding

The Student Review Committee (SRC) provides financial support for VL2 Student Network Members to attend professional and academic workshops, meetings, or conferences in which they may present their own work and/or learn more about relevant issues in their field of work.  It also provides support for students who wish to gain acdded training in research methodologies or technology (i.e., but visiting a different laboratory or undertaking a specialized skills course).  Lastly, the SRC supports students wishing to initiate, conduct, and/or complete their research projects, in which they will distribute their findings through publications or conferences.  In short, the SRC funds these types of activities:
• Travel to workshops, meetings, or conferences
• Training in new research methodologies
• Initiating or continuing research projects

To see the complete SRC guidelines for 2016–2017, click here.

Questions about SRC funding should be directed to the SRC Chair of the Student Leadership Team (SLT).  You can find the information for the current SRC Chair on our contact page.

Maximum monetary allotments are established at the beginning of each academic year and my change from time to time.  Therefore, it is important that you review the complete SRC guidelines for the academic year in which you are interested in applying.  Successful grant applications could be awarded:
• Research funds for computer equipment and participants to complete a dissertation-level study
• Funding for travel, hotel, and conference registration for a meeting or conference relative to your field of work
• Funding for travel and per diem to a VL2-affiliated laboratory to gain experience in a specific line of work
• Tuition costs for an online course in a specific topic not offerred by your university
